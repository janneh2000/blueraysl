About Blue-Ray

An indigenous African company with global standards — headquartered in Freetown, expanding across West Africa, and delivering construction, mining and engineering at every scale.

Our Story

From local roots to a regional force.

Blue-Ray (SL) Ltd. was incorporated and registered with the Government of the Republic of Sierra Leone in 2016 as a Premier Company — born from a simple conviction: Sierra Leone's infrastructure should be built by Sierra Leoneans, supported by the best partners the world has to offer.

Over the years we have concentrated our effort on mobilising the right professionals, skilled and unskilled labourers, equipment and tools — and on identifying credible partners and financiers to bring international capacity to local projects.

Today, Blue-Ray is delivering quality service across construction, consulting, general supplies and mineral exporting — and is a trusted contractor for several Sierra Leonean government agencies. Tomorrow, we are taking that same standard across West Africa.
