  • Patching & repair

Bridges & Drainage

Buildings

Infrastructure

Service 02

Mining & Mineral Export

Sierra Leone is rich in minerals — and Blue-Ray helps unlock that potential responsibly. We carry out exploration and extraction operations and manage the export logistics that connect Sierra Leonean minerals to the global market, working only with vetted, licensed buyers.

  • ▸ Mineral exploration & site assessment
  • ▸ Extraction operations & site management
  • ▸ Mineral processing & preparation
  • ▸ Export logistics & international shipping
  • ▸ Regulatory compliance & reporting

Engineering & Survey Services

Engineering Surveys

Aerial & Topographic

Hydrographic Surveys

Land Information

Foundation set-out and reinforcement
