Service 02

Mining & Mineral Export

West Africa sits on some of the richest mineral deposits in the world — and Sierra Leone is one of its most important sources. Blue-Ray's mining division is structured to unlock that potential responsibly: from exploration and extraction, through processing and logistics, to direct export to vetted international buyers.

We work hand-in-hand with the National Minerals Agency, the Environment Protection Agency Sierra Leone (EPA-SL), local chiefdoms and host communities — because real mineral wealth is the kind that benefits the country it comes from.

Minerals We Work With

Sierra Leone and the wider West African region are home to a remarkable range of strategic minerals. Below are the categories we are positioned to extract, process and export.

💎
Diamonds
Industrial & gem-quality alluvial diamonds — Sierra Leone is one of the world's premier diamond sources.
🥇
Gold
Alluvial and primary gold from licensed concessions across the Sierra Leone greenstone belt.
⛓️
Iron Ore
High-grade iron ore — Sierra Leone hosts globally significant deposits at Tonkolili and Marampa.
🪨
Bauxite
Aluminium-bearing ore feeding global smelters — substantial reserves across the region.
⚫
Rutile & Ilmenite
Titanium-mineral sands — Sierra Leone is among the world's leading producers of natural rutile.
🏗️
Construction Aggregates
Granite, laterite and gravel for infrastructure projects across West Africa.

End-to-End Mining Capability

One company, one accountable team, from licence to logistics.

01

Exploration & Assessment

Extraction & Site Management

04

Processing & Preparation

05

Export & Logistics

Containerisation and shipping through Sierra Leone Port Authority — direct to vetted, licensed international buyers.

06

Community & Environment

Local hire, environmental rehabilitation and benefit agreements with chiefdoms and host communities.
