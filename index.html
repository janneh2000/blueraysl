Who We Are

An indigenous force, scaling across West Africa.

Blue-Ray (SL) Ltd. is a fully registered indigenous Sierra Leonean company, incorporated in 2016 with one mission: to contribute meaningfully to the infrastructural development of our nation — and now, our region. We mobilise the best local and expatriate professionals, heavy equipment and credible international financiers to deliver roads, bridges, buildings and mining operations on time and on budget.

We never promise more than we can deliver — but we always deliver more than we promise. That principle has earned us long-standing relationships with the Sierra Leone Roads Authority, the Ministry of Health & Sanitation, the Sierra Leone Port Authority and the National Commission for Social Action — and is the foundation we are building our regional expansion across Liberia, Guinea, Ghana, Senegal and Nigeria on.
